Marina Lorusso: "The Art of Typography"

Explore the visual symphony of fonts and styles as Marina Lorusso takes you on a captivating journey through the nuanced world of typographic design. Uncover the artistry behind letters and discover how they shape our visual experiences.

Don Norman: "Designing for an Ageing World"

Don Norman, a luminary in user-friendly design, reflects on the challenges faced by an aging population and urges designers to create functional and stylish solutions. Join the discourse on inclusive design that transcends age boundaries.

Natasha Iskander: "Design Thinking's Conservatism"

Natasha Iskander challenges the status quo, questioning the foundations of design thinking. Delve into her critique, exploring whether this widely embraced approach truly fosters innovation or, as Iskander suggests, inadvertently preserves existing structures.
